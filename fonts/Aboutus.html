ABOUT US

Segun Johnson Associates Limited (SJA) is a company that deals in Construction of Building and Civil Engineering Projects, Food production(processing, production, packaging and exportation), Paint distribution and other items such as gympsum board, and also, we into Sales services such as Lands and houses. SJA discover that there is need for proper management of services to our Clients. We involve in these services in order to translate the desires of our Clients into a stable and aesthetic structure at an affordable cost within the stipulated period.

VALUE

Our values, which are reliability, credibility, responsiveness and commitment, are the bases for everything we do. They guide the way that we manage our business and clients by giving them prompt and satisfactory services at affordable price.

MISSION STATEMENT

Is to enhance safety, durability and aesthetical value to our Clients projects or services, and improve on the management culture of our clients' infrastructure. To Add Value to their developments as they expand their businesses within and outside the country, rendering our services at an affordable value.


VISION

Our Vision is to be a reliable, customer friendly, solution provider company in all industry that we are involved, solving our client's problem, what they want, how they want it and when they want it, all to their satisfaction.
