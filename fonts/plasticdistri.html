SJA PAINTS DEPOT

is a subsidiary of Segun Johnson Associates Limited, a paint distributorship company that deals in distribution of high quality paints in SHIELD AND DNMEYER, coupled with a professional application of our paints for our esteemed clients. With the quality and benefits of these paints at our disposal and availability of markets within the country, we discovered that there is huge opportunities in this area of paint and painting business.

SJA PAINTS has a depot, which is a show room with the paints samples which includes SHIELDS (Easy living and Excel), DNMeyer (Imperial and Ultimate) and high end feature wall paints FINEDECO.

MISSION STATEMENT

Our Mission is to enhance the growth of quality paints within our region and Nigeria as a whole, in order to achieve qualitative, durable and artistic finishing to our projects and its attendant benefits as our main focus.

VISION

Our Vision is to be a distributor of good and quality paints and offer our clients a professional services and continue to be a reliable and customer friendly company that we are to our Clients.

ESTABLISHMENT

This Depot company came into existence in the month of November 2015, and commence business in November 2015 as SJA Paint Depot (a subsidiary of Segun Johnson Associates Limited).

QUALITY CONTROL

The company takes into consideration the control of quality from the production stage to ensure the batches are same colour and standard. Also, we ensure that the paints are applied professionally in order to give desired finishing and satisfaction.

PACKAGING

The Paints are packaged in gallons and drums of 4 liters and 20 liters respectively.

PAINTS DISTRIBUTORSHIP

SJA Paint Depot distributes two main quality paints, SHIELD and DNMeyer.

1) SHIELD PAINTS: This paint comes in two different categories, they are:-

- EASYLIVING which is a medium brand, it is a good quality paint which is mostly used for internal finishing on wet and dry walls. It is fortified with light fast pigments which gives it a smooth finish after its application. This quality of paint is affordable and odourless, it covers well more that its pears of same level.

- EXCEL: This is the premium brand of Shield paint. It has a higher quality because it is produced with fast base of high grade acrylic dispersion. It is highly pigmented with quality alkali resistant pigments which allows it to dry to a smooth desirable finishing that is washable, prevent moulds and fungi growth on applied walls. It is good for both external and internal walls be it wet or dry. It has a very high standard that competes with other high quality paints.

2) DNMEYER PAINTS: This paint comes in two different categories, they are

- IMPERIAL which is the standard category of the DNMeyer paint. It is of medium quality which comes in emulsion and gloss.

- ULTIMATE which is the premium category of DNMeyer paint is of a premium quality which comes in emulsion, gloss and textured. It covers well giving smooth and perfect finish.
