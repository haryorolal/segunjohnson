SJA FOODS

is a food processing company that deals in processing, production, packaging and exportation of foods. With the quality, benefits and quantity of food products within our country Nigeria and availability of markets inside and outside the country, we discovered that there is huge opportunities in this area of agro-allied business. We have presently in our production line Plaintain Flour, Roasted peanut, Peanut butter, Beans flour, Dried fish, Frozen snail etc.

Benefits of roasted peanuts (groundnuts), snails, beans flour and other food products cannot be over-emphasized and our products are subjected to NAFDAC laboratory analysis and we have approval with the number inscribed on the products along with Food and Drug Agency of United State of America registration number (See Bottle label)

MISSION STATEMENT

Our Mission is to enhance the growth of quality processed food within and outside Nigeria with safety, durability and health benefits of our products as our main focus.

VISION

Our Vision is to be a global producer and exporter of African foods (processed and natural), a reliable and customer friendly company within Nigeria and around the world.

VALUES

Our Values, which are reliability, credibility, responsiveness and commitment, are the bases for everything we do. They guide the way that we manage our businesses and customers by giving them prompt and satisfactory attention.

Food Processing, Production & Packaging

SJA Foods engages in processing, production and packaging of Nigerian Foods which marketed to stores within and outside the country.

Products in stable of SJA Foods are:
      SJA Foods Bottled Groundnuts. (Sizes 600g, 300g & 230g) Net Weight Only
      SJA Foods Peanut Butter(Size: 500g) Net Weight Only
      SJA Foods Beans Flour. (Sizes: 1kg & 500g) Net Weight Only
      SJA Foods Unripe Plantain Flour(Sizes: 1kg & 500g)
      SJA Foods Dried Fish (Size: Depends on Customers)
      SJA Frozen Snails (Size: Depends on Customers)

The Processing, Production, Packaging and Exporting department came into existence in the month of October 2015, but commence operation in August 2016 as SJA Foods (a subsidiary of Segun Johnson Associates Limited).

FACTORY

SJA FOODS has a factory of 4 bedroom flat with a big Reception room to receive correspondence from customers and meetings. There is a Cloak room for changing of outfit to factory fittings, there is also a Raw materials room where the pet bottles and cocks are packed for safe keeping. The Production rooms have all respective machines from roasting to peeling machines, milling and grinding machine along with mixing machine. Packaging and Labeling room has scales, funnels, capping machine, coding machine, sealing machine and other tools that are used and labels to the products. The factory is now located at #24, Lekan Salami Street, Salami Estate, New Bodija, Ibadan, Oyo State.

QUALITY CONTROL

The company takes into upmost consideration the quality control of products from the purchase of materials, production to packaging of the products. There is proper fumigation of the factory twice in 30 days, laboratory coats, factory shoes, hand gloves, nose/mouth mask, head gear and other fittings within the factory for all staff.

PACKAGING

Our products are well packaged. The peanuts are packed into pet bottles of different sizes ranging from 230g to 600g. After bottling, the products are taken to the Labeling room for capping and labeling. The peanut butter are packed in a clear bottle of size 500g. The snails and bean powder are packed into neatly sealed transparent plastic bags with clear labels.
